Two types of Airbnb Hosts

Who is the host behind Airbnb? I once lived in an Airbnb the host is a old lady with a lot of books, we talked and talked about a lot of books, of course a nerd like me felt like it is the best part of my trip. But in other times, I lived in an Airbnb where I never met the host and it felt like a self-checked hotel.

So the point is host is really important part of Airbnb and they essentially the creator of the experience. By playing around with the Boston Airbnb data given in Kaggle (https://www.kaggle.com/airbnb/boston) I found there are two types of Airbnb host: Those with a plenty of property to rent (has total listings more than 3), and those who only rent their own house.

In this article, we only tried to understand more about the professional host the individual host, besides how much listings they have. The questions I try to answer are:

  • Is the professional host tends to charge more than individual host>
  • Is the professional host required longer minimum stay nights than individual host?
  • Is the professional host has more availability in 30 days than the individual host?
  • Is professional host has better reviews than the individual host?

Is the professional host tends to charge more than individual host?

Our result said yes! On average professional host charge 186.92, while individual host charge 164.91. you can see from the chart that there are more professional host in the 200-400 price range than individual host.

Is the professional host required longer minimum stay nights than individual host?

Our result say yes! A Large portion of professional host required guest to stay more than 3 nights.

Is the professional host has more availability in 30 days than the individual host?

Absolutely yes! At the time the data is scraped, most of individual host has availability less than 10 days in 30 days, whereas many professional host still has a plenty availability.

Is professional host has better reviews than the individual host?

Our result say no! Even though most of the professional and individual host lies in the range of 80-100, you can see from the chart, individual host receive higher score than professional host.
